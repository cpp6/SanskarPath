+# SanskaarPath 🕉️
## Ancient Values. Modern Minds. Balanced Futures.

---

### 🌟 Project Vision
**SanskaarPath** is a revolutionary digital wellbeing and education platform designed to combat mobile addiction in children aged 4–16. Instead of "banning" technology, we transform it into a structured, gamified, and value-based learning journey that builds character and discipline.

---

### 🔴 The Problem
- **Passive Consumption**: Children average 4-6 hours of mindless scrolling on platforms dominated by non-educational or addictive content.
- **Mental Health**: Increasing dopamine addiction and decreasing attention spans.
- **Parental Worry**: Zero control over content structure and time spent on recreational apps.

### ✅ The Solution
A curated, parent-controlled ecosystem that incentivizes learning through **Sanskaar (Values)**.
- **Structured Content**: Only approved, age-appropriate educational videos.
- **Negative Reinforcement of Addiction**: Hard screen time limits and auto-lock features.
- **Positive Reinforcement of Values**: XP rewards, badges, and levels for completing lessons.

### ✨ Key Features

#### 1. 🧒 Child Dashboard (The "Playroom")
- **Personalized Path**: Content automatically filtered by age group (4-6, 7-9, 10-12, 13+).
- **Gamification Engine**: Earn XP, level up, and unlock **12+ custom badges** (e.g., "Early Bird", "Wisdom Seeker").
- **Focus Mode**: Encourages offline activities when digital time is exhausted.
- **Value Quotes**: Daily inspirational quotes from ancient wisdom (Vedas, Gita, etc.).

#### 2. 🛡️ Parental Control (The "Command Center")
- **Remote Lock**: Instantly lock the child's dashboard from the parent login.
- **Screen Time Management**: Set daily limits and track real-time usage.
- **Analytics**: Beautiful charts (Recharts) visualizing progress and learning hours.

#### 3. 🛡️ Admin Panel
- **Cloudinary Integration**: Admins can upload MP4 lessons directly; the system auto-tags and optimizes them.
- **Approval Workflow**: Manual verification of content before it goes live for different age groups.

---

### 🧠 Smart Logic (Innovation Points)
- **Dynamic Age Engine**: A proprietary logic that re-evaluates content delivery as children grow.
- **Streaming Buffer Logic**: Custom Cloudinary streaming implementation to ensure smooth playback even on low-bandwidth rural connections.
- **Serverless DB Connection**: Optimized MongoDB connection pooling for ultra-fast response times on edge functions.
